Fitness Management System

Sep 2024 - Oct 2024 System Design UI/UX DESIGN & FRONT-END DEVELOPMENT

Project Overview

With the growing popularity of health and fitness, managing fitness center operations—such as member data, class schedules, bookings, and progress tracking—can be complex and inefficient. This project develops a comprehensive online fitness management system that streamlines these tasks, allowing visitors to view fitness and course information, members to register and manage bookings, trainers to create courses and track their schedules, and administrators to manage the website, update course details, and approve member registrations efficiently.

Goals & Objectives

  • Develop a fitness management system that efficiently handles all data related to fitness operations.
  • Allow users to register as fitness members, providing their information and selecting membership packages that suit their needs.
  • Enable members to book training courses, view their booked schedules, and cancel reservations if needed.
  • Allow trainers to update their availability for course scheduling and view their own work schedules.
  • Enable administrators to manage member data (approve, view, delete), manage trainers (add, view, delete), manage training programs (add, delete), and manage training sessions by assigning trainer availability to different programs.
