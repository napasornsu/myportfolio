Phone Number Checking Application

Jan 2024 - Feb 2024 Mobile App Design

Project Overview

Nowadays, phone scams are increasingly common, causing a large number of people to become victims. These scams include being tricked into transferring money by someone claiming to be an official, impersonation by acquaintances to request help, or being persuaded to perform various financial transactions.

To address this issue, the Phone number checking application is proposed. This application allows users to verify unknown phone numbers to see if they have a history of scams before deciding to answer calls or conduct any financial transactions. The app aims to reduce risks and enhance the safety of mobile phone usage for the public.

Goals & Objectives

  • Monitor and search for information on unknown phone numbers to be aware of potential scammers.
  • Serve as a decision-making aid before conducting any transactions.
  • Prevent the risk of being scammed.
