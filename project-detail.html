Customer Feedback and Info Management System for Vimandin Spa

Feb 2024 - Mar 2024 Web Design & Development

Project Overview

Vimandin Spa is a holistic wellness center located behind Khon Kaen University. The spa offers a wide range of services such as herbal steam therapy, traditional Thai massage, body spa, facial spa, aromatherapy massage, as well as food and beverage services. It also serves as a training center for Thai massage and spa careers.

Currently, the spa shares its information only through a Facebook page, which is not sufficient for accessibility and credibility. Therefore, the spa requires a website system that can store basic information and customer reviews, allowing customers to easily find details while also serving as an additional channel for advertising and promotion.

Goals & Objectives

  • Develop a website system to store and display the spa's information, enabling customers to easily access and explore details about the services.
  • Provide an additional advertising and promotional channel beyond the existing Facebook page, increasing the spa's online presence and accessibility.
